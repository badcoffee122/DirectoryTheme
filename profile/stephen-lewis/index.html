Biography

Stephen Lewis has established himself as a distinguished attorney in New York, specializing in general and criminal defense law. With a career spanning over 15 years, Stephen has garnered a reputation for his thorough understanding of the law, commitment to his clients, and a keen ability to navigate complex legal scenarios.

After graduating from Columbia Law School, Stephen began his legal journey, quickly adapting to the dynamic nature of general law while developing a keen interest in criminal defense. His passion for justice and advocacy led him to work on numerous high-profile cases, where he demonstrated exceptional skill in crafting robust defense strategies.

Stephen’s approach is grounded in a deep respect for the law and a commitment to protecting the rights of his clients. He works closely with each client, offering personalized attention and crafting tailored solutions to meet their unique legal needs. His dedication to his profession extends beyond the courtroom, as he regularly engages in community outreach and legal education programs, sharing his knowledge and experience with others.

Stephen’s areas of expertise include DUI/DWI defense, drug-related offenses, white-collar crimes, and general civil litigation. His ability to handle cases with compassion and precision has earned him both respect and trust among his peers and clients alike.
